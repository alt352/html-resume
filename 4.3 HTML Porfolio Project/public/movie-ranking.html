All Time Favorite Movies

ALT Top 3 Movie Favorites

1. Spirit

Spirit is an animated tale about a young horse who gets separated from his mother and captured by early settlers and his fight to break free and find his herd again.

2. A Bronx Tale

A Bronx Tale is a love story/ tragedy about an Italian gang and a young Italian boy who falls in love with a black girl in the late 1960s.

3. Django

Django is a fictional tale about a slave who became a freeman and journeyed across the South with the bounty hunter as his partner looking to find and buy his wife's freedom.
